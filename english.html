Book of poem

Girl from a camomile

This book has been in the making since my little girl was born. Poem by poem I wrote, sang it, like a lullaby, in a game, imagining our future adventures. I put all my emotions into these words... The most beautiful energy is sent into the lyrics, a lot of love: true, unconditional, endless... I wish you to enjoy it as much as I do. That your children learn to sing, read, write with these songs, that their days are full of fun and nights full of beautiful dreams... Find an inspiration in yourself, your children, this book; to create... I hope that you will agree with me that LOVE IS ABOVE ALL...
